Kesar Mango

The Kesar Mango, also called the Queen of Mangoes, is popular for its sweet, bright orange pulp and exquisite taste. In south Gujarat, the Kesar mangoes ripen late in the summer in the months of June. In our Orchards, the process of growing , ripening and harvesting the mangoes, is completely organic and natural to make sure, our customers get the most delightful fruit from nature.
