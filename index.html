KESAR MANGOES

Our mangoes were plucked from an organically maintained orchard with the world class infrastructure. The orchard is located at Aat , in the Indian state of Gujarat. Aat is a small village, situated on the southern-western outskirts of Navsari. The village covers an area of 7 square kilometres and is only 4kms from the Arabian sea, exactly at 20°53'1"N 72°51'34"E.

We believe that, the Orchard maintenance, soil health and intercropping plays a big role in the health of the plant. After all the Queen of Mangoes requires patience and timely upkeep to get the best quality of the fruit.

About Us

A Global provider of Organic Kesar Mangoes

Our love for mangoes launched the search for real taste of mangoes. This made us develop our own Orchard to be able to grow the best organic, naturally ripened Kesar Mangoes.

We understand how Indians based in India, USA, Canada and other western countries aspire their mangoes to be unadulterated and organic. Aat Nu Kesar was created to share the awesome natural, organic taste of Kesar Mangoes from the south Gujarat region. Our Orchards excludes the use of weedicides, pesticides and fertilizers.
